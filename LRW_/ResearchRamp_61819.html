    Program Checking: Prepare Stage
  • Understand the survey from a respondent point of view.
  • Think about how to enhance the survey to get better data.
  • What is program checking? Once a questionnaire is final. We programm into a computer format. Into Online and CAWI (Interviewer or self-administered in person)
  • Why do we program check? Impress clients, Cleaning Data, Improve user experience
  • What do i need? Final Questionnare, Survey Link Envrionment, Program Checking guide, JIRA Portal or External Document to track Change
    Program Checking guide
  • Step 1: Determine the diferent paths or question sequences in your program
  • Step 2: For each path determine what screener/main questionnaire responses are neccessary to get that path
  • Step 3: Create a spreadshet listing question responses necessary to get each path as determined in steps 1 and 2
  • Step 4: assign paths to different people who will check the program
